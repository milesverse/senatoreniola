About

I'm Senator Eniola, a Junior Software Developer from Lagos, Nigeria. I'm passionate about building modern, user friendly web applications that solve real world problems.

I recently earned my BSc in Computer Science from Ladoke Akintola University of Technology, and I’ve already had the opportunity to work with real teams and real users through multiple internships and freelance projects.

Currently, I’m interning again at Empocreat (The EOC), where I help manage MoveMoni, a web-based tool that helps users find the best exchange rates from providers like LemFi and Taptap Send. I work on scraping automation, workflow improvements, and UI enhancements using tools like n8n, APIs, and browser dev tools.

Previously, I worked at Straight Gate Publishers where I maintained and optimized their WordPress site, handled backend content updates, and even stepped up as the designer, creating marketing visuals and social media content to help promote published books.

My goal? To grow into a highly skilled software developer, keep learning from every project, and contribute to meaningful tech, whether that’s in Web3, open source, or startups.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy designing, writing about tech on Medium, and learning something new every day. I’m always looking for opportunities to collaborate on exciting projects, so feel free to reach out if you think we could work together!
